Kartikaeya Kumar


I am an undergraduate student at the Indian Institute of Technology(IIT), Guwahati, majoring in Electronics & Electrical Engineering(EEE) with a minor in Computer Science. I am broadly interested in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) & Machine Learning. My research focuses on designing systems that leverage existing techniques in ML, CV, NLP, Graphics, etc., to enrich social experiences and/or enhance human productivity & creativity.

Currently, I am working as a research intern at the Creativity Lab in UC San Diego, advised by Prof. Haijun Xia. Before this, I was a MITACS research intern working with Prof. Tony Tang at the RICELab at the University of Toronto. I have also worked at the UE-HCI lab at IITG and the VIGIL lab at IITH.
